User manual for VizMan Group

The organization admin can also be a group admin if the organization admin chose yes in Are you a group organization while setting up a company and added a group name. He or she can add all of the VizMan-using companies, that are also the part of Group.

If the admin selected yes in group company during setup, the group icon will appear on the dashboard, and the admin will be able to add his or her VizSafe companies to the group by clicking on it.

Adding Office

The group admin must follow these steps to add any organisation to the group.

Step 1: Select the Add button and enter the organization's admin's mobile number.

Step 2: After clicking Add, the details of the organisation will be displayed in masked form, and after approving the addition, the organisation will be added to the list.

Step 3: The organization's details will be masked until it has been validated.

Step 4: To verify the information, click the verify button, and an OTP will be issued to the organization's admin's mobile number.

Step 5: Enter the right OTP, and the organization's details will be properly displayed, and the group admin will be able to access various reports for the organisation.

Note: The group can include organisations that have been registered and confirmed with VizMan.

Dashboard & reports

The group admin can access a variety of reports and filter them by the organisation. By clicking on the organisation at the top of the panel, the group admin can switch to his or her organisation.

In reports, selecting dates is required in order to view the reports and these are also filtered based on organisations.
